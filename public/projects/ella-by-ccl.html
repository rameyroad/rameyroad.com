ELLA by CCL

ELLA is a personalized AI-powered leadership development assistant, backed by over 50 years of proven research and real-world application from the Center for Creative Leadership (CCL). ELLA learns to anticipate your unique leadership development needs and provides just-in-time assistance along your journey to become a better leader.

  • Prepare for your day: CHAT with ELLA about upcoming challenges. Practical and actionable ADVICE is at your fingertips.
  • Leverage free moments for learning: Browse TOPICS in ELLA to expand your leadership skills and stay up-to-date on emerging trends in leadership development.
  • Take time for reflection: Learn from a difficult situation or tackle a new challenge using ELLA’s image-aided REFLECT feature.
  • Accelerate your development: Experience proven, interactive EXERCISES to learn and practice new skills and behaviors, such as effectively giving actionable feedback or listening for understanding.

Ask ELLA:

  • How can I prepare myself for a promotion?
  • How can I get people excited about my ideas?
  • How can I communicate better with my boss?

ELLA is always with you and always available, ready to support your leadership growth at every stage.

*ELLA is available as an enterprise application through the Center for Creative Leadership and can be downloaded for iOS or Android.

Technologies used:

  • Microsoft .Net Core 6.x, .Net Web APIs
  • React Native & Expo GO
  • Entity Framework Core, Entity Framework, & Azure SQL Server
  • REST development & integration
  • Azure DevOps build, deployment & web hosting
